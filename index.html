How it works

From individual intent to collective force.

01
Set the condition

A campaign defines a specific, verifiable action — a policy change, a certification, a commitment — that an organization must take.

02
Make your pledge

You commit to donate, purchase, or act. Your card is authorized but never charged — until the condition is met. No action from them, no cost to you.

03
Watch it ignite

When the organization acts, the pledges fire. Thousands of commitments become real — at once. That's leverage that can't be ignored.
